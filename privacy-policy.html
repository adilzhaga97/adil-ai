01 — Overview

About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Adil AI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our Instagram messaging service powered by artificial intelligence.

By messaging our Instagram account, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

02 — Data We Collect

Information We Collect

When you interact with our Instagram DM service, we may collect the following information:

  • Your Instagram username and profile information (public profile name, profile picture)
  • The content of messages you send to our account
  • Timestamps of your messages and our responses
  • Your Instagram User ID (assigned by Meta/Instagram)
  • Technical metadata associated with message delivery

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or any data beyond what is shared through Instagram's messaging platform.

03 — How We Use Data

Use of Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your direct messages using our AI system
  • To improve the quality and accuracy of automated responses
  • To detect and prevent abuse or misuse of the service
  • To comply with applicable laws and Meta's platform policies

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing direct message responses.

04 — Data Sharing

Third-Party Services

To operate this service, your message data may be processed by the following third-party providers:

  • Meta / Instagram — the platform through which messages are received and sent
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI model used to generate responses to your messages
  • n8n — workflow automation platform used to process and route messages

Each third party operates under its own privacy policy. We do not sell your data to any parties.

05 — Data Retention

How Long We Keep Your Data

Message data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations.

  • Active conversation data: retained for up to 30 days
  • Aggregated, anonymized usage logs: up to 12 months
  • Data subject to legal hold: as required by applicable law

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 08).

06 — Your Rights

Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Objection — object to certain processing of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the details in Section 08. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

07 — Security

How We Protect Your Data

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Access to message data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review our data handling practices and security measures

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
